Overview

Founded in 2017 by Changpeng Zhao, Binance has grown to become the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume. Headquartered globally, the platform serves millions of users across 100+ countries.

Fees & Pricing

Spot Trading Fee0.1% (maker & taker)

Using BNB (Binance Coin) to pay fees reduces your trading costs by 25%. High-volume VIP tiers offer further discounts.

Security & Safety

Binance takes security seriously with multiple layers of protection:

  • SAFU Fund: Secure Asset Fund for Users holds over $1 billion to cover potential breaches.
  • Proof of Reserves: Published on-chain via Merkle tree proofs for full transparency.
  • 2FA: Supports hardware keys, Google Authenticator, and SMS.
  • Cold Storage: Majority of user funds stored offline.

Key Features

  • Spot Trading: 350+ trading pairs with advanced charting.
  • Futures & Options: Up to 125x leverage on major pairs.
  • Staking: Earn passive income on 100+ supported assets.
  • Copy Trading: Follow top traders automatically.
  • Launchpad: Access to new token launches.
  • Binance Card: Crypto debit card for spending.

User Experience

Binance offers both a simplified "Lite" mode for beginners and a full-featured "Pro" mode. The mobile app is well-designed with TradingView-powered charts and real-time notifications.

Our Verdict

Binance remains the top choice for traders who want the widest coin selection and the lowest fees. The interface may be complex for beginners, but its Lite mode and educational resources help bridge the gap.

FAQ

Is Binance safe to use?
Yes. Binance has one of the largest security budgets in the industry, maintains a $1B+ insurance fund (SAFU), and publishes proof-of-reserves audits regularly.
Is Binance available in the United States?
Binance.com is not available in the US. American users must use Binance.US, which has fewer features and coin listings.
What are the trading fees on Binance?
Standard spot trading fees are 0.1% for both makers and takers. Paying with BNB gives a 25% discount (0.075%). Higher volume VIP tiers offer further reductions.
How do I reduce Binance trading fees?
Hold BNB in your account and enable BNB fee payment. Trade in larger volumes to reach VIP tiers. Use limit orders (maker) which have lower fees than market orders (taker).
